Welcome To Paintiles Construction PTY LTD

In 2018, Paintiles Construction which is a footprint in the construction sector was created with the aim to improve both private and public sector. To further advance its massive contribution to the Southern African civil engineering and infrastructure development, as well as the aspirations of the Southern Africa region. Paintiles Construction is 100% black owned with the aim to assist in eradication of poverty and also help with skill transferance in the construction sector. Since its establishment, Paintiles Construction has been in the fore front in the home improvements and have been able to provide services such as Tilling, Painting, Plumbing, Roofing, Kitchen Units, Kitchen Tops,laminated Flooring, Built in-Cupboards and Ceilings. The success of Paintiles Construction (Pty) Ltd is an indication on the level of confidence South Africa has placed on Paintiles Construction (Pty) Ltd technical expertise, safety and quality standards in construction and infrastructure management of projects. Building your home can be one of the most stressful yet satisfying projects to undertake. And likely your single biggest financial investment. With over 5 years in construction, we know the tricks of the trade and will walk you through the process. From foundations to occupation and everything in-between. We manage each stage of the build; from site establishment to wet-works and finishes.
